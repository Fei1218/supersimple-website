Welcome to Our Farm Shop

The farm shop has a wide range of fresh products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon. Our pork is reared in the fields around our house, giving it a distinctive flavour.

Our Café & Shop

Enjoy your purchases immediately in our small café attached to the farm shop. We offer fresh eggs, milk, yogurt, cheese, bread, cakes, and much more.

Activities at the Farm

Join us for hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze. Visit the animals, play with farm tools, or pick your own produce from the garden!
